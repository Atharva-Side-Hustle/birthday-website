Happy Mother's Day to Aai, Aaji, and Archana Maushi

Today I just want to say thank you to the three most important women in my life.

Aai, I miss you every single day. Being away from home has made me realize just how much your presence means to me. I miss your voice, your small reminders, and just having you around. Nothing really fills that space but knowing you are always there for me keeps me going.

Aaji, तुझं साधं विचारणं — "दूध पिलास का", "नाश्ता केलास का", "जेवला का" — एवढंच किती आपुलकीने भरलेलं असतं.
तुझं बोलणं आणि सोबत राहताना वाटतं की घर काही दूर नाही.
तुझ्यासारखं प्रेम दुसऱ्या कुणाकडून मिळणं कठीण आहे.

Archana Maushi, you've made this new place feel more like home without even trying. Whether it's your care, or just the way you look out for me, it's always felt like I have a second parent here.

Thank you all for being there for me in your own ways. I love you and I'm so lucky to have you.
Wishing you a very happy Mother's Day.
